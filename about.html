The Founder

Grayson Kube

Founder — Venom Industries LLC · Colorado, USA

I'm a self-taught developer. I didn't come through a CS program or a bootcamp — I learned by building things, breaking them, and figuring out why. That process taught me something most formal education skips: the gap between "it works" and "it works well" is enormous — and most teams never close it because nobody showed them where to look.

I built Venom Industries because I kept seeing the same problems on site after site: unoptimized images killing Core Web Vitals, CVEs sitting in production dependencies for months, pages that Google couldn't even index. Real problems with real business impact — and no clear owner.

The Venom-Audit is the product I wish existed when I was debugging someone else's mess. A complete technical picture, a prioritized fix list, and a human to walk you through it. Beyond audits — I build software and SaaS products with the same standards. And soon, the VENOM approach comes to automotive.
