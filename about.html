About us

We build software for people who play to win.

Venom Industries is an indie software company. We build sharp, focused tools that give founders, marketers, and sales teams an information edge over the competition.

Why we exist

Information asymmetry is the most valuable edge in business. The people who know more — about their competitors, their prospects, the market — win more. That's not a theory. It's just how business works.

Most of that intelligence is sitting right there on the open web. Every website your competitor runs tells you exactly what they're investing in, what's working, and how they operate. The problem is nobody has time to manually dig through it.

We automate that. We build tools that surface the signal instantly — so you can stop operating on gut instinct and start making decisions based on real data.

We're not a venture-backed startup optimizing for hockey-stick growth metrics. We're an independent company that builds software we'd want to use ourselves, prices it honestly, and supports customers like humans.

What we believe
Software should do one thing extremely well
We'd rather ship one sharp tool than a bloated platform. Focus compounds.
Privacy is non-negotiable
We build products where user data stays with the user. Your browsing is yours. Always.
Honest pricing, no tricks
No fake discounts, no dark patterns. You see what it costs. You decide. Done.
Ship fast, support well
Small team means short feedback loops. When something breaks or something's needed, we move.
