I'm a self-taught developer based in Colorado. I didn't come up through a CS program or a bootcamp — I learned by building things, breaking them, and figuring out why.

That process taught me something most formal education skips: the gap between "it works" and "it works well" is enormous — and most teams never close it because nobody ever showed them where to look.

I built Venom Industries because I kept seeing the same problems on site after site: unoptimized images killing Core Web Vitals, CVEs sitting in production dependencies for months, pages that Google couldn't even index. Real problems with real business impact — and no clear owner.

SaaS Detective is the product I kept wishing existed every time I visited a site and wanted to know what it was running. A free Chrome extension that reveals the full tech stack in one click — 80+ tools, 30+ categories, zero data collected. That's it.
