PRIVACY
POLICY.

Last updated: March 14, 2026 · Venom Industries LLC · Colorado, United States

The short version: We collect only what we need to deliver your audit and communicate with you. We don't sell your data. We don't share it with third parties for marketing. We're a small operation and we treat your information the way we'd want ours treated.

WHO WE ARE

Venom Industries LLC is a technical audit company based in Colorado, United States. We operate venom-industries.com and the SaaS Detective Chrome extension at saas-detective.venom-industries.com. This policy covers both properties.

For questions about this policy, contact us at grayson@venom-industries.com.

WHAT WE COLLECT

Information you give us directly

When you fill out our booking form or contact us, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your company name and website URL
  • Your tech stack and the concerns you share with us
  • How you found us

We use this information solely to deliver your audit, communicate about your project, and send you payment information. We do not use it for marketing without your explicit consent.

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, our server logs may record your IP address, browser type, referring page, and pages visited. This data is used only for security monitoring and basic analytics. We do not use advertising trackers or sell this data.

Payment information

Payments are processed through Stripe. We never see or store your full card number. Stripe's privacy policy governs how your payment information is handled — you can review it at stripe.com/privacy.

Form submissions

Our contact form is powered by Formspree. When you submit a form, your data is processed and forwarded to us via Formspree. Formspree's privacy policy governs that processing — you can review it at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

SAAS DETECTIVE EXTENSION

SaaS Detective is a Chrome extension that detects tech stacks on websites you visit. It operates with a strict zero-data-collection policy:

  • All detection runs locally in your browser. No data about the sites you visit is ever transmitted to our servers.
  • We collect zero browsing data. We cannot see which sites you visit, what tools were detected, or how you use the extension.
  • No account is required. The extension works entirely without authentication or registration.
  • No analytics are embedded in the extension itself.

The extension's only network activity is checking for updates via the Chrome Web Store, which is governed by Google's privacy policy.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver your audit report and communicate about your project
  • Process payments and send invoices
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Improve our services based on aggregate, anonymized feedback

We do not use your information for advertising, sell it to third parties, or share it with anyone other than the service providers listed above (Stripe, Formspree) who are necessary to deliver our services.

DATA RETENTION

We retain your contact and project information for as long as necessary to deliver your audit and for up to 2 years afterward for business records. You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us.

YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • The right to access the personal information we hold about you
  • The right to correct inaccurate information
  • The right to request deletion of your information
  • The right to object to how we process your information

To exercise any of these rights, email us at grayson@venom-industries.com. We'll respond within 30 days.

COOKIES

Our website does not use tracking cookies. We do not run advertising networks or remarketing pixels. If we ever add any form of cookie tracking, we will update this policy and provide appropriate notice.

SECURITY

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including HTTPS encryption on all pages. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take this seriously and keep our systems updated.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If we make significant changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page. For material changes, we'll make reasonable efforts to notify you if you have an active engagement with us.

QUESTIONS?

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your information, reach out directly.
