Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. We're transparent about how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Last Updated: September 24, 2025

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use VENOM-INDUSTRIES services, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Data: Name, profile picture, preferences
  • Subscription Data: Billing information, subscription plan, payment history
  • Career Tooling Data: Career information, work history, skills, education details

Technical Information

  • Device Information: Hardware specifications, operating system, browser type
  • Network Data: IP address, connection quality metrics (for performance optimization)
  • Usage Analytics: Feature usage, performance metrics, error logs
  • Cookies: Session tokens, preference settings, analytics data

VENOM-INDUSTRIES does not and will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party services (payment processors, cloud hosting) under strict data protection agreements

  • Data Backup: Secure, encrypted backups with geographic redundancy

Organizational Measures

  • Regular security training for all team members
  • Incident response procedures for data breaches
  • Regular third-party security assessments
  • Compliance with industry security standards

  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (with some exceptions)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format

Communication Preferences

  • Opt-out of marketing communications at any time
  • Manage notification preferences in your account settings

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account Data: Until account deletion + 30 days for security purposes
  • Subscription Records: 7 years for tax and financial compliance
  • Resume Data: Until you delete it or close your account
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated and anonymized after 24 months
  • Support Communications: 3 years for service improvement

International Data Transfers

VENOM-INDUSTRIES operates globally. Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place:

  • Data processing agreements with all service providers
  • Compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy laws
  • Regular audits of international data handling practices
  • Use of approved transfer mechanisms (Standard Contractual Clauses)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we do:

  • We'll post the updated policy on our website
  • We'll notify you via email if changes are significant
  • We'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Your continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
