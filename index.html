How It Works

Three seconds from
curious to informed.

Browse Any Site

Visit any website you're curious about — a competitor, a prospect, a business you admire. SaaS Detective works on every page across the entire web.

Click the SaaS Detective icon in your toolbar. The extension silently scans the page's scripts, stylesheets, and global variables in real time — all locally.
