How it works

THREE SECONDS FROM

No setup, no account, no data sent anywhere. Just click and know.

Visit any website you're curious about — a competitor, a customer, a business you admire. SaaS Detective works on every page across the entire web.

Click the SaaS Detective icon in your toolbar. The extension silently scans the page's scripts, stylesheets, and global variables in real time — all locally.
